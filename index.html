About

I am an undergraduate student at Sharif University of Technology specializing in Computational Geometry and Reconfiguration Algorithms. My interests include Graph Algorithms and Computer Vision, alongside solving challenging mathematical and computational problems.

Research

Reconfiguration of Lattice-based Sliding Squares

Collaborating with Hugo Alves Akitaya, this project explores efficient reconfiguration of modular robotic systems using meta-modules while maintaining connectivity. Developed a parallel algorithm for reconfiguration using sliding moves.
Papers:

  • Finding Shortest Reconfiguration Sequences for Modular Robots (accepted at FWCG 2024).
  • Parallel Sliding Squares (submitted to SOCG 2025).

Graph Threading

Working with Rebecca Lin, this research addresses graph threading with turn costs in 3D grid graphs. Developed lower bounds, hardness proofs, and approximation algorithms for graph threading problems.

Quasi-Twisting Doubly-Covered Rectangles

Investigating the quasi-twisting of doubly-covered rectangles into different polyhedral forms under the guidance of Alireza Zarei. The project involves identifying quasigeodesics on convex rectangles.
Paper: Preparing submission for EuroCG 2025.
